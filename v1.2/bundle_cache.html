bundle cache

Cache all the gems to vendor/cache 
$ bundle cache [--no-prune] [--all]

Options:

--no-prune: Don't remove stale gems from the cache.

--all: cache :git, :path, and .gem dependencies. Once used, the --all option will be remembered.
